Vasantham TV Appearance

Nayantara Parpia was featured on a TV show "Enna Nadakkuthu" (meaning What's Happening) on the Tamil TV Channel Vasantham in Singapore. The show features unique arts activities that take place in the city. This episode aired on 27th April, 2015.
